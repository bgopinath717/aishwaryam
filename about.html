About Aishwaryam

A caring home built on dignity & love

Aishwaryam Old Age Home Care is a premium senior living community in Manapakkam, Chennai. We provide a warm, family-oriented environment where elders enjoy comfort, companionship, medical support and the respect they deserve.

Our Mission

To provide quality elderly care with dignity, compassion and respect — creating a true home where every senior feels valued, safe and loved.

Our Vision

To be Chennai's most trusted senior living community, setting the benchmark for warmth, comfort and holistic care for the elderly.

Core Values

Compassion, integrity, dignity and family. We treat every resident the way we would care for our own parents.

Meet Our Founder

Dr. B. M. Venkatraman, M.D. (Philippines)

Founder – Clinic, Old Age Home & Educational Institution

Dr. B. M. Venkatraman is the visionary Founder of our Clinic, Old Age Home, and Educational Institution, dedicated to serving society through healthcare, elder care, and education. Having earned his M.D. degree from the Philippines, he brings decades of medical expertise, compassionate leadership, and an unwavering commitment to community welfare.

Driven by the belief that quality healthcare, dignified elder care, and accessible education are the foundations of a progressive society, Dr. Venkatraman established these institutions to create a lasting and meaningful impact on people's lives. Through his dedication and service, he has positively influenced countless individuals and families across generations.

Beyond his professional accomplishments, Dr. Venkatraman is widely respected for his compassion and generosity. At his Old Age Home, he personally supports and cares for some residents at his own expense, ensuring they receive proper accommodation, nutrition, medical attention, and a dignified quality of life. He firmly believes that no elderly person should be deprived of care due to financial difficulties and continues to extend support to residents who are unable to afford the full cost of their stay.

His commitment to social welfare extends to education as well. Through his Educational Institution, he has helped deserving students pursue their studies by offering free education to some students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds. Believing that financial hardship should never become a barrier to learning, he continues to support students who are unable to meet educational expenses, helping them build a brighter future.

His leadership, integrity, and passion for excellence continue to guide our mission of delivering exceptional medical care, providing a nurturing environment for senior citizens, and fostering quality education for future generations.

Today, Dr. Venkatraman's legacy stands as a symbol of compassion, service, and social responsibility, inspiring us to improve lives, strengthen communities, and uphold the highest standards of care and education. His selfless dedication to helping those in need continues to make a meaningful difference in the lives of many, reflecting his lifelong commitment to humanity and community welfare.
